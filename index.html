Why Calisthenics?


Calisthenics are affordable and convenient for all those involved. They can be done anywhere there is enough space to carry out the exercises, including outdoors!. These exercises enable a high number of calories to be burnt as they require a lot of movement from multiple muscle groups at once. This in turn may help to build muscle, lose fat and gain strength.


Calisthenics can improve:
  • Strength
  • Balance
  • Flexibility
  • Endurance
  • Coordination
