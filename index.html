What is Calisthenics?


Calisthenics is a type of exercise that involves using a person's body-weight to carry out a variety of movements which involves very little or zero equipment. These exercises stimulate a variety of large muscle groups with movements such as jump squats, pull ups and push ups.


Why Calisthenics?


Calisthenics are affordable and convenient for all those involved. They can be done anywhere there is enough space to carry out the exercises. These exercises enable a high number of calories to be burnt as they require a lot of movement from multiple muscle groups at once. This in turn may help to build muscle, lose fat and gain strength.


Calisthenics can improve:


  • Strength
  • Balance
  • Flexibility
  • Endurance
  • Coordination
